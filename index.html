Dataset from "GGMDDC: A Multilingual Dataset for Audio Deepfake Detection"

Abstract: In today's era, criminals have developed advanced deepfake methods to perform ill activities. Deepfakes severely threaten the security of audio, videos, and images, amd hence efforts are made to develop a robust system for Audio Deepfake Detection (ADD). However, they fail to develop a reliable system due to advancements in deepfake audio creations. Many datasets also exist for ADD tasks, however, limited to a low language number count, their usage remains limited to restricted speakers. Also, in most of the existing datasets for ADD, the issue of some speakers' real and deepfake systems exists. In this study, we implemented a modified version of GAN, namely, HiFi-GAN, to create this dataset, namely, GAN-Guideded Multilingual Corpus for Deepfake Detection (GGMDDC). We performed several experiments on this dataset, which gave highest accuracy of 99.78 \% for ADD task.

For more details of our work, please refer to the paper.
Our implementation is available in the github repository.
